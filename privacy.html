Privacy Policy

Effective July 17, 2026.

The Short Version

10b10t does not want your data. There is no registration, no email address, no analytics tracking, and nothing is ever sold or shared with advertisers. What little the server touches is listed below.

IP Addresses

10b10t never stores player IP addresses. No IP logs are kept.

Your IP does, however, pass through services that need it to function while you are connected:

  • The authentication server, which handles offline-mode logins.
  • Anti-bot protection, which filters join floods and bot attacks.
  • The DDoS protection nodes that proxy your connection to the server.

These services use your IP live to do their job. Once you disconnect, the server keeps no record of it.

What Is Stored

  • Your username and UUID.
  • Your auth password, stored hashed, never in plain text. The server's security is taken extremely seriously; still, as with any service, do not reuse a password you use anywhere else.
  • Normal Minecraft world data: inventory, position, stats, and builds.
  • Playtime, which gates dupes and other features.
  • Chat logs.

Chat Is Public

In-game chat is logged and streamed live on this website for anyone to read. The online player list, with usernames and skin heads, is also shown on the website. If you do not want something public, do not type it in chat.

Third-Party Services

Some things are handled by third parties with their own privacy policies:

  • Donations go through the shop site. Payment details are handled by the payment processor there; 10b10t never sees your card details.
  • The website loads its font from cdnfonts.com and player skin heads from crafatar.com and mc-heads.net, so your browser makes requests to those services when you visit.
  • The Discord server and YouTube channel are governed by Discord's and Google's own policies.

The One Exception

As stated in the Terms of Service, CSAM is the one case where the no-storage habit is set aside: whatever evidence is available at the time, including connection data from an active session, will be captured, preserved, and included in the report to the authorities.
